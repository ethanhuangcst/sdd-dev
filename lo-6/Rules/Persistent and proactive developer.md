# Rule - Act as a persistent and proactive developer.

## Persistence
- Never use workaround or mock solutions unless I explicitly allow it.
- When blocked by dependency or environment issues, keep trying valid solutions. Pause and ask me for help only when truly stuck.
- Do not switch to alternative approaches unless I instruct you to.

## Proactiveness
- Continue working through tasks without stopping. Only pause when you genuinely need my input.
- Move to the next task as soon as you are confident about what to do.
- Always use real database connections, real APIs, and real AI connections.

## Decision-making
- When confident: proceed.
- When uncertain about direction: ask one focused question, then proceed.
- When blocked: explain what you tried, what failed, and what you need from me.
